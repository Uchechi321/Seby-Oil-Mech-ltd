Technical & Advisory Services

We are a group of articulated engineering and field development professionals with extensive experience in well engineering, production and reservoir engineering, and geological analysis. Our expertise spans drilling, completion, workover, recompletions, and rig-less interventions.

Drilling Services

  • Preparations for onshore and offshore drilling operations
  • AFE planning, budgeting, and performance processes with NUPRC (NNPCL) and clients
  • Risk analysis and drilling performance optimization
  • Effective completion designs to achieve production targets
  • Work evaluation, bid evaluation, and cost tracking
  • Planning and management of drilling materials, equipment, and tools
  • Drilling contracting processes

Workover Services

  • Detailed analysis of wells requiring intervention
  • Technical support for retrieval of permanent dual packers
  • Selection of effective remedial services for water shut-off
  • Completion materials planning and management
  • Recommendation of suitable sand control methods

Rig-less Intervention

  • Well integrity analysis and mitigation strategies
  • Development of programs to return wells to production
  • Production optimization initiatives
  • Identification and harnessing of low-hanging production opportunities

Logistics & Materials Management

  • Management of field logistics activities
  • Quality assurance and quality control (QA/QC)
  • Expediting and warehousing coordination

Completion Accessories

  • Pup joints and tubing
  • Blast joints and dual packers
  • Infill string packers and production packers
  • Wire wrap screens

Wells, Reservoir & Facility Management

  • Integrated production optimization to increase asset value
  • Asset technical data analysis and data gap closure
  • Data acquisition, interpretation, and integrated production modelling
  • Field studies, appraisal plans, field development plans, and WRFM planning

Asset Management

  • End-to-end asset management covering the full production chain up to point of sales

Based on the volume of drilling, completion, workover, and recompletion activities across multiple fields, we propose the creation of advisory roles that holistically manage and guide safe operations. This approach minimizes lost opportunity time (LOT/NPT) and delivers measurable performance and cost-efficiency improvements.
